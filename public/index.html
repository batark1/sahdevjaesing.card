Brief about:

Sahdev Jaesing is a performance-driven coach and multidisciplinary creator with over five years of experience in fitness training, athletic development, and physique transformation. He has trained a wide range of clients—from injury rehabilitation cases to professional boxers focused on building strength, agility, and reflexes.

With a strong foundation in anatomy, nutrition, and performance-enhancing methods, Sahdev designs personalized programs that integrate bodybuilding, calisthenics, powerlifting, and combat conditioning. He is a Junior State-Level Bodybuilding Champion (2024) and is known for his sharp observation, discipline, and ability to build systems that drive long-term results.

Beyond fitness, Sahdev is also a creator and strategist—developing tools and resources around design, human behavior, and performance psychology to help others evolve physically and mentally.

As a creative, Sahdev blends aesthetics, emotion, and identity through visuals—crafting content that moves with rhythm and intention. His work spans styling, modeling, and even garment creation, often curating his own wardrobe pieces as expressions of philosophy and form.

He is also a digital tinkerer — building custom solutions through underrated and looked over tech pieces, constantly merging physical presence with digital identity. Whether it's painting, music, writing, or developing a new tool, his creative pursuits are rooted in clarity, transformation, and expression.

At the core of everything, Sahdev is a thinker — a modern-day polymath evolving in public.
